VIP Desert Safari Dubai: An Adventure Begins

VIP desert safari Dubai provides a unique way to explore the desert. The private 4×4 vehicle and dedicated guide ensure that you have a personalized experience that is tailored to your needs. The drivers of the four-wheel-drive vehicles are experts in navigating the challenging terrain, ensuring that you have a safe and enjoyable ride. Witness the Beauty of Sunset and Enjoy Refreshments

As you journey through the desert, you will have the opportunity to witness the beautiful sunset, with its vibrant colors casting a beautiful glow across the sky. You can take in the stunning view while enjoying some refreshments provided by VIP Dubai tours. This is an excellent opportunity to relax and take in the beauty of the surroundings.

Bedouin-Style Camp

The Evening Desert Safari includes a stop at a Bedouin-style camp where you can enjoy traditional Arabic food and beverages, including shisha and coffee. The camp is an excellent place to relax and take in the beauty of the desert. You can watch traditional entertainment such as belly dancing and tanoura shows or participate in activities such as camel rides, sandboarding, and henna painting. This is a great opportunity to learn about the Bedouin culture and their way of life.

Morning Desert Safari

Many people don’t have time to prepare for an evening safari because of their hectic schedules. Thus, they can take part in an exciting adventure tour, a Morning Desert Safari. With the desert safari, you may enjoy quad bikes, camel rides, and sand skiing in addition to roughly 20 minutes of thrilling dune bashing and an electrifying camp adventure. Depending on your vacation package, the morning safari may change. Several businesses provide roughly a 2-hour package trip on average. For those who are not interested in camping and dining, it is the best choice.

Overnight Desert Safari

For those seeking a unique experience, an Overnight Desert Safari is highly recommended. While everyone is sound asleep at the campsite, take a nighttime tour of the Arabian Desert. Don’t forget to bring a camera to capture the sunrise over Dubai’s sandy desert. This overnight desert adventure includes discovering henna art, trying on traditional attire, and going on camel rides.
