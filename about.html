About Us

Desert Safari Dubai offers a wide range of all inclusive packages for different kinds of desert safaris with many additional options, such as off-road adventure seekers like dune buggies and quad bikes with skilled professional guides. We can customize your desert safari requirements as we have many camps. The camps are personally selected by our desert safari experts and are clean and hygienic. All our cars are 4×4 and our drivers are experts and licensed. We are the most preferred online agent for desert safari bookings, as we don’t believe in making an extra buck just because we have a chance.
Do send us your query and we will respond within 2 hours. We can also support in all ground services in Dubai including hotels, sightseeing and car rentals. We have dedicated staff members to take care of your queries 24/7.

Do pick up and drop off times vary for the desert safari?

Yes, pick and drop timings change monthly depending on the sunrise and sunset timings.


What clothing is recommended for the desert?

Comfortable clothing along with lots of sunscreen is recommended. Also do carry cap/hat, scarf, sunglasses and a camera. Jackets are recommended during the winter months.
